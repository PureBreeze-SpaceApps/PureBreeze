An Air Filtration Unit: Purifying Air, Protecting Lives

"In the spirit of Apollo 13, we did it with what we had." Inspired by ISS technology, We built an air purifier with inexpensive parts and measured the input and output with a Raspberry Pi and sensors.

About Our Project

We created a product inspired by International Space Station technology that cleans the air of an enclosed space and eradicates pathogens.
